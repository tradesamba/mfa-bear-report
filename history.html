Profiles

All three are generated every run. Win-rate sells further-OTM calls (highest probability, smallest credit). Payoff sells closer (richer credit, lower probability). Balanced is the middle. The delta band and credit/width floor are financially coupled — see the guide §5. POP is delta-implied (theoretical, capped 85%), not a measured win rate; bear call spreads have asymmetric payoff (max loss > max gain). Close before earnings.
