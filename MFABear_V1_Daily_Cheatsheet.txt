WHAT THIS DOES — READ FIRST
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Finds the TOP 4 tickers each day to open a BEAR CALL SPREAD (sell an OTM call, buy a higher call;
collect a credit). You WIN if the stock stays BELOW the short strike at expiry (flat / down /
mildly up). You LOSE if it rallies through. Defined-risk, NEGATIVE SKEW: max loss > max gain, so a
HIGH WIN RATE is essential and OVER-rejecting is cheap, UNDER-rejecting is ruinous.

This is the MIRROR of MFA V6 (which buys bullish breakouts). Same deterministic data layer + Veto #8
integrity gate; the screen direction, scoring, vetoes, and the option-chain step are bear-oriented.

OLD MFA V6 FLOW:  Layer 0 (long screen) → Grok sentiment → Claude scoring → Top 4 longs
NEW MFA BEAR:     Layer 0 (bear screen + real option chain) → Grok upside-risk → Claude confirm → Top 4 BCS

THE WIN-RATE ↔ PAYOFF CHOICE (--profile) — PICK ONE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
A safer (low-delta) short strike wins more often but collects less credit. You cannot maximize both.
 PROFILE    short Δ band   CWR floor   ~POP     character
 winrate*   0.13–0.20      ≥ 0.09      ~80–85%  highest win rate (default; "success rate paramount")
